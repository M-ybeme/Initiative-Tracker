Design Principles

  • Offline-first: Everything runs locally in your browser.
  • No accounts: No logins, no subscriptions. Your data belongs to you.
  • Fast: Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript. No frameworks, no bloat.
  • Integrated: Tools talk to each other for minimal friction.
  • DM-centric: Built around real needs at the table.
  • Private: No analytics, no tracking.

How It Works

  • Characters, maps, journals, and settings are saved using IndexedDB.
  • Your data never leaves your device.
  • Import/export lets you back up or move content.
  • Works offline after first load.
  • Cross-tab sync keeps multiple windows in sync.

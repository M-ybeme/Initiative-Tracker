Name Generator

Quick Start: Pick a race, click "Apply Race Preset", then "Generate"

Customize syllables, gender leaning, and flavor (Harshness, Exoticness). Toggle Surname for two-part names.

  • Per-tile actions: 📋 copy, ⭐ favorite, ↻ re-roll (double-click too), 🔒 lock
  • Lock names you want to keep — re-generate refills only unlocked slots
  • Press Enter anywhere (outside a field) to generate instantly
  • Settings/results auto-save. Use Share to copy a link

Results

  • Harshness increases consonant clusters (kr, gr, zz).
  • Exoticness increases apostrophes and diacritics (á, ê, ü).
  • Race presets set sensible defaults; tweak as needed.

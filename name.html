Name Generator

How to Use the Name Generator

Pick a Style , set Count , adjust syllables , gender leaning , alliteration , and special characters. Click Generate.

  • Copy copies all results to your clipboard.
  • Download saves a .txt.
  • Seed makes your results repeatable (same seed ⇒ same list).
  • Custom Tables lets you add/override styles with JSON (see the guide inside).
